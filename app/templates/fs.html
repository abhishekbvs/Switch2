Rules and Regulations*

  1. A team consisting of 8-20 members ( technical and non technical) are permitted to register for the show. (A maximum of 18 students are expected to walk the ramp)
  2. A set time limit of 10 minutes shall be provided for each team (including set up time and performance).
  3. The team exceeding the time limit shall be given negative marking.
  4. Greenrooms shall be provided for changing purpose (mirrors and other requirements should be brought by the participants).
  5. The team should carry their tracks in pendrive format and submit at the time of on sight registration.
  6. Usage of cigarettes, alcohol or any abusive substance shall lead to direct disqualification.
  7. Vulgarity is strongly prohibited, any form of obscenity in the team shall lead to debarring of the whole team from the contest.
  8. Coordinators of the participating team is required to send the pictures of the costumes and writeup of their theme before 11th of March 2019.
  9. Participating members are expected to bring along their respective student identity cards at their time of insight registration.
  10. They are also bound to submit a scanned copy and bring their respective college's Bonafide certificate with a seal at the time of online and on sight registration respectively.
  11. Props such as oil, water or any other flammable substance that can damage the floor should be kindly avoided at any cost.
  12. All decisions made by the judges shall be final.
