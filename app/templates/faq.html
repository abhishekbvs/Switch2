FAQ


What to do if I am not redirected to the payment gateway? We are sorry you faced such an issue. Please try again by going back to the registration page. I paid the money but the transaction failed. We’ll update / refund the amount and promptly inform you as per the situation when the bank notifies. How long will I be able to wait after clicking register, before finishing payment? We assign a 20 minute lock period for completing your payment. In case you faced some issues and came back after the 20 minutes, we’ll still register you without delay if slots are available. Otherwise, your registration amount will be refunded and you’ll be duly informed. Is there any other payments option? Please contact us directly at the support email / number. Is Vidyut account compulsory for registering for an event (competition / workshop / cultural / flagships)? Yes it is. We identify and map you based on your Vidyut ID.
